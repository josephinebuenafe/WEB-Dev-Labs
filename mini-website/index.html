Welcome to the Philippine Festivals Guide

The Philippines is famous for its colorful, energetic, and culturally rich festivals. Each fiesta showcases the joy, artistry, and traditions of Filipinos. From grand street dances to elaborate floats, every festival tells a unique story. Whether you are a tourist or a local, this guide will help you explore 15 of the country’s most celebrated festivals.

Top 15 Festivals in the Philippines

  • Sinulog Festival – Cebu City
  • Ati-Atihan Festival – Kalibo, Aklan
  • Panagbenga Festival – Baguio City
  • Kadayawan Festival – Davao City
  • Pahiyas Festival – Lucban, Quezon
  • MassKara Festival – Bacolod City
  • Moriones Festival – Marinduque
  • Pintados Festival – Tacloban, Leyte
  • Higantes Festival – Angono, Rizal
  • Dinagyang Festival – Iloilo City
  • Obando Fertility Rites – Bulacan
  • Giant Lantern Festival – Pampanga
  • Sandugo Festival – Bohol
  • Ibalong Festival – Legazpi, Albay
  • Kaamulan Festival – Bukidnon
